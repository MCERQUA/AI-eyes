SPRAY FOAM MARKET STATISTICS & INDUSTRY DATA

MARKET SIZE & GROWTH:
- Global spray foam market: $2.646 billion in 2024
- Projected to reach $5.763 billion by 2035
- Compound Annual Growth Rate: 7.33% (2025-2035)
- Spray foam insulation market: $2.181.5 million in 2025
- Expected to grow to $4.452.1 million by 2035
- CAGR for insulation market: 6.7% (2025-2035)
- Industry growth driven by energy efficiency demands and building retrofits

MARKET TRENDS:
- Retrofit and renovation projects - biggest growth segment
- Shift toward sustainable, low-VOC foam formulations
- Smart building integration with spray foam systems
- Advanced thermal mapping applications
- Enhanced application methods and equipment
- Focus on climate-friendly blowing agents (HFO replacing HFC)
- Building code requirements increasing foam demand
- Residential market growing faster than commercial
- DIY segment expanding with user-friendly products

INDUSTRY DRIVERS:
- Rising energy costs and efficiency mandates
- Government incentives for energy-efficient buildings
- Stricter building codes for insulation performance
- Consumer demand for comfort and lower utility bills
- Aging building stock requiring retrofitting
- Increased awareness of thermal bridging issues
- Technological advancements in foam application
- Environmental concerns driving sustainable formulations
