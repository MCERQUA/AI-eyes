PI-GUY MOBILE BASE RESEARCH FINDINGS - 30 Nov 2025

MAIN PLATFORM OPTIONS:
1. DiffBot (GitHub - ros-mobile-robots/diffbot)
   - Two wheel differential drive
   - Raspberry Pi 4 B compatible
   - ROS Noetic middleware on Ubuntu Mate 20.04
   - Complete ROS driver packages available
   - Real robot and simulation configs
   - Website: ros-mobile-robots.com

2. ROMR (ROS-based open-source mobile robot)
   - Research and industrial applications
   - Detailed hardware design documentation
   - ScienceDirect paper with full specs
   - More advanced than commercial platforms

3. ROSbot 2R
   - Raspberry Pi 4 ARM64 based
   - Autonomous open source robot platform
   - Reinforced with tutorials and simulation models
   - Great for prototyping autonomous vehicles

3D PRINTING COMPONENT SOURCES:
- Thingiverse: Millions of 3D models and files
- Printables.com: ESP32 Robot Car Chassis - modular & open source
- STLBase: Search engine for STL files (273+ OpenSCAD robot files)
- bjepson/Bot-Chassis: OpenSCAD 3D-printable bot chassis
   - Includes body.scad, wheel.scad, servo bracket.scad
   - Modular design for customization

SPECIFIC PROJECTS WITH STL FILES:
- ESP32 Robot Car Chassis - designed for DIY and education
- 4WD robotics car chassis with Arduino yellow motors
- HELENE: Six-axis closed-loop control robot (6 DOF)
